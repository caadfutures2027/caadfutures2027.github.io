Social events are integrated into the conference program to support informal interaction and community building among participants.

Welcome ReceptionDay 2 evening — following the workshop exchange session.

Paper Sessions

Paper presentations will take place across Days 3–5. Accepted papers will be organized into thematic sessions aligned with the conference topics. Detailed session assignments will be published after the review process is complete.

Workshops

Workshops will run across Days 1–2 and take place in SAPL's dedicated maker spaces, woodshops, and robotic fabrication labs. Workshop proposals are due February 2027, with acceptance notifications in May 2027.
